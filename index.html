Data Collection

App Functionality Data

The Age Calculator app performs all date calculations entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data entered into the app, including dates of birth or any other dates you enter for calculations. All calculations are performed locally on your device and no data is sent to our servers.

Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

Our app displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalised advertisements. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, operating system and browser information
  • App usage information for ad targeting purposes

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. For more information about how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalised advertising by adjusting your device settings under Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Usage Data

Basic usage data may be collected automatically by Google Play Services and AdMob, including device information and app interaction data for analytics and advertising purposes. We do not have direct access to or control over this data beyond what Google provides through their developer tools.

Use of Your Data

The limited data collected through our app is used solely for:

  • Serving advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Improving app performance through anonymised analytics

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since we do not collect personal calculation data, your dates and personal information remain entirely on your device. For advertising data handled by Google AdMob, Google applies industry-standard security measures.
